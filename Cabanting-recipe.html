Garlic Buttered Shrimp Recipe

Garlic Butter Shrimp is a super easy dish to make. This recipe shows how to cook this beloved dish the Filipino way. I like how it turned out. The dish is rich and flavorful. I enjoyed eating it with newly cooked white rice

About This Recipe

This simple and delicious garlic buttered shrimp recipe is packed with flavor and takes just 15 minutes to prepare. The combination of garlic, butter, and shrimp creates a mouthwatering dish perfect for any occasion.

Procedure

  1. Heat olive oil and 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until the shrimp turns pink and opaque.
  4. Reduce heat to low, then add the remaining butter and lemon juice. Stir until the butter is melted and the shrimp is well-coated.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.
  6. Serve the garlic butter shrimp immediately with your favorite side dishes, such as pasta, rice, or a fresh salad.
